What about us?

Overview

  Cohasset Sports Complex opened its doors in 2010. We have both a full sized outdoor turf field and an indoor turf field used to offer a variety of camps, clinics and leagues for people to get involved. Our activities range from training toddler's soccer or lacrosse skills to adults wanting to get some practice in too!

  Our indoor space is top-of-the-line turf, which can be used as a full sized field, 2 mid sized fields for league play or 4 smaller training fields. The indoor facility also contains garage doors that can be opened or closed depending on the season.

Facilities

  At Galway Rovers Football Club, our mission is to develop players from Junior Rovers (U5) through High School (U19). We create their success through proper technique, discipline, competitive soccer… and fun! Our coaches are carefully selected for each age group to train players to gain tactical awareness and reach their highest potential.

  The Galway Rovers Football Club is the hidden gem of the South Shore, in operation for the past 37 years. We are the most family-friendly soccer club and provide amazing life experiences beyond the pitch. See how you can become part of our family and join us at our club!

Our system

  In your initial interview we will assess your physical condition through a study of the body composition (bone%, water, fat, muscle) and we will make a training tailored to your goals TOTALLY FREE

  Then you will have continuous technical advice in the fitness room, changing your program when it completes its cycle … At Atenas we make it very easy for you to be in shape.

  If you still prefer to accelerate the process and make it more specific and motivating consult our Personal Training service.
